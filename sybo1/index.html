یک عکس معمولی و واضح از محصول بگیر

کیفیت عکس مرجع روی حفظ جزئیات محصول اثر مستقیم دارد.

  • محصول کامل داخل کادر باشد.
  • نور کافی و رنگ واقعی محصول مشخص باشد.
  • لوگو، دوخت، زیپ، دکمه و جزئیات مهم واضح باشند.
  • برای لباس، عکس کامل ست یا خود لباس را استفاده کن.
نکته: هدف تغییر محصول نیست؛ همان محصول واقعی باید حرفه‌ای‌تر نمایش داده شود.

عکس را به ChatGPT بده و این پرامپت را کپی کن

برای حفظ هویت محصول و ساخت فریم تبلیغاتی حرفه‌ای.

پرامپت ساخت تصویر تبلیغاتی
Use the uploaded product photo as the STRICT and ABSOLUTE source-of-truth reference for the real product.

CRITICAL PRODUCT PRESERVATION:
The product in the final image must remain exactly the same real product shown in the uploaded reference.

Preserve with maximum possible accuracy:
- exact design and silhouette
- exact proportions
- exact colors
- exact fabric and material appearance
- exact texture
- exact stitching and seams
- exact buttons, zippers, pockets and hardware
- exact patterns, prints, logos, labels and branding
- every visible product detail

Do NOT redesign the product.
Do NOT change its color.
Do NOT add or remove product features.
Do NOT replace logos or branding.
Do NOT create a different version of the product.

Create an ultra-realistic premium fashion advertising image.

SCENE:
An attractive adult fashion model is wearing the exact uploaded product in a sophisticated modern dressing area before going out to an important evening event.

The environment is elegant, contemporary and realistic:
- premium modern interior
- large mirror
- subtle architectural lighting
- dark neutral colors
- soft warm practical lights
- tasteful cinematic atmosphere

The model makes a final natural adjustment to the exact product while looking confident and stylish.
The product must be the visual hero of the image.
The pose should feel candid and effortless, not like a traditional catalog pose.

CAMERA:
Vertical 9:16 composition.
Cinematic fashion campaign photography.
Medium-full body framing.
35mm cinematic lens feeling.
Shallow depth of field.
Natural realistic skin.
High-end commercial lighting.
Realistic fabric physics and folds.
Photorealistic premium fashion advertising quality.

Keep the entire product clearly visible.
Do not crop important product details.

No text.
No captions.
No additional logos.
No fantasy elements.
No distorted hands or body.
No artificial plastic skin.
قبل از مرحله بعد: رنگ، فرم و جزئیات محصول را با عکس اصلی مقایسه کن. اگر تغییر کرده، تصویر را دوباره بساز.

پرامپت کامل Google Flow
Use the uploaded product image as the strict visual reference for the clothing/product.

Create a fast-paced, ultra-realistic cinematic vertical 9:16 fashion advertisement.

IMPORTANT PRODUCT CONSISTENCY:
Keep the exact same product throughout the entire video. Preserve the overall design, colors, materials and recognizable details. Do not redesign or change the product.

The model is an attractive adult fashion model.

STORY:
Start immediately with a fast cinematic fashion montage.

The model quickly gets ready and puts on the product/outfit. Show this through stylish rapid match cuts rather than a slow dressing process:
- grabs the product/outfit
- quick transition: wearing it
- adjusts the outfit naturally
- quick close-up of product details and fabric
- final mirror check

Keep this preparation sequence fast, energetic and visually satisfying.

Then immediately transition to the same model confidently entering a stylish modern evening party.

The party should feel premium, social and realistic, with adult guests, elegant atmospheric lighting and a fashionable modern environment.

The model walks through the party wearing the exact same product/outfit.

Another stylish adult guest naturally notices the outfit, approaches and asks in Persian:
«این استایلو از کجا خریدی؟»

The model smiles naturally and replies in Persian:
«از آنلاین‌شاپ سایبو.»

IMPORTANT SPEECH:
Both speakers must speak fluent natural Persian with a contemporary Tehran accent. The conversation should feel spontaneous and realistic, like two friends talking at a party.

MUSIC:
Add energetic premium fashion music from the very beginning. Use modern cinematic electronic fashion music with a stylish beat. Lower the music naturally during dialogue and raise it again after the final line.

CAMERA:
Fast cinematic match cuts during preparation. Dynamic close-ups of product details and fabric. Smooth tracking shot entering the party. Natural medium shot during the conversation.

STYLE:
Ultra-realistic.
Premium fashion campaign.
Modern Instagram Reel aesthetic.
Realistic skin and fabric.
High-end commercial cinematography.

No subtitles.
No captions.
No random text.
No product redesign.
No distorted hands or bodies.
No unnecessary scene changes.
